Portfolio

V8 Engine Model

SolidWorks Side Project

To improve my CAD and analysis skills, I challenged myself to learn and design a V8 engine model from videos and images on the web. I plan on trying to eventually develop this into a 3-D model that I can print and showcase! Currently I have most of the engine block with a cross-plane crank shaft and functioning pistons.

Extruded Li-Ion Battery Design

Property of KPM Power

This is one of the battery designs I created during my time at KPM. It was designed to fit inside all walkie forklifts. It has an extruded case with gaskets to have a sealed but accessable interior for service. It was designed with Solidworks with tolerances in mind. One of the things I really enjoyed doing was designing the details of the product. Wires (some not shown) were necessary to determine lengths in electric cable schematics for production.

UWAFT Mechanical Team

NX - Design Team

UWAFT participates in the 4-year long Ecocar challenge. The goal is to take a market car, gut it, and turn it into a hybrid. During my time on the team, I designed mounts and casings for differents parts of the car such as pumps and cameras. I also created HV junction boxes for the electrical components fitting on small areas of the car CAD. I participated in vehicle architecture selection by research and analysis of components.

DEG Prototype Test Rig

Property of Nova Institute

Apart from the heavy electrical research, I also did mechanical design and calculations to develop a budget tight testing rig to stretch the DEG (dielectric elastomer generator). Research on motors and applying correct gearing to get the desired force was critical for this project.

Robot in 3 Days

Design Team

Ri3D is a 72-hour building challenge for people who are no longer eligible to participate in FIRST. The nature of the challenge is to make a functioning robot the moment the challenge is announced by FIRST. This means that all the ideas are original and all the tasks need to be completed efficiently ideas are original. I manufactured aluminum parts for the milk-crate carriage and assembled the elevator system.

FIRST Robotics

Team 854

For a few years in high school, I participated in FIRST because a good friend of mine convinced me to see if I liked it. And I did. Immensely. So much so that I decided to take a degree to pursue the field of robots. During my time on the team, not only did I foster my enthusiam for engineering but I also gained valuable skills such as manufacturing and designing that I use and polish to this day.

Guitar Playing Robot

First Year Design Project

For my final project of my first term of university, I worked with three friends to create a lego robot that could translate guitar tabs from the internet and play them on a guitar. I primarily did the mechanical side of the project and did a lot of the building but I also wrote code to make the frets play. Fun fact, I learned how to play guitar because I thought it would be fun and might help with the design of the robot.
