Welcome! I'm Ben,
an aspiring engineer and a current Mechatronics Engineering Student at the University of Waterloo. Thanks for checking out my site and getting to know me. The snow on the homepage was not created by myself.

I love designing solutions and prototyping. I believe that solutions should be robust, intuitive, and elegant.
Ever since I was small, I started to design by transforming my 2D drawings into 3D Lego builds. Along with building giant snowforts with tunnels and chairs in the recess time of harsh Canadian winters and the reign of older kids (gotta protect your fort with slush), I really became a hands-on thinker that loved to imagine what could be.

My current goals are to learn how to design for more manufacturing processes (vacuum forming and injection molding), how to design more organic components in CAD software, get better at surface modelling, and learn how to perform FEA.

If you're looking for a design freak that knows how to design around constraints and manufactuability, please contact me at: ben.duo99@gmail.com

I'd like to consider myself to be a pretty well-rounded person, and I have many interests. I enjoy working with people and I'm not afraid to recieve the harsh truth or give it out either. I like to think that my friends can describe me as kind, funny, and smart. But none of them would like to admit it I think...
Below I'll introduce myself further and to my hobbies and I guess you can judge for yourself!
