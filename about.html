Welcome! I'm Ben,
an aspiring engineer and a current Mechatronics Engineering Student at the University of Waterloo. Thanks for checking out my site and getting to know me. The snow on the homepage was not created by myself.

I love designing solutions and prototyping. I believe that solutions should be robust, intuitive, and elegant.
Ever since I was small, I started to design by transforming my 2D drawings into 3D Lego builds. Along with building giant snowforts with tunnels and chairs in the recess time of harsh Canadian winters (and the reign of older kids), I really became a hands-on thinker that loved to imagine what could be.

If you're looking for a design freak that knows how to design around constraints and manufactuability, please contact me at: ben.duo99@gmail.com

Staying Active
I think trying to stay healthy and fit is really important for my well being and my ability to work and think at my best. During school I enjoy partaking in various sports. I enjoy doing sports due to their competitive nature and just their overall fun. Every term, I join the badminton club and play more 2/3 of their weekly sessions. I also do various intramurals such as Ultimate Frisbee and Indoor Volleyball where I enjoy the team atmosphere. I also try and go to the gym when I can, especially during co-op.
